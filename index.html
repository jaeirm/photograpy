Technology Reimagined: Image Generation Challenge

About the Event

Step into the world where technology and artistry intersect! This event challenges participants to harness the power of Bing Image Creator, an advanced AI tool based on DALL-E, to generate images similar to reference images provided on the spot. Your goal? To replicate the reference image with precision while showcasing your creative flair and speed on prompting.
Participants: individuals

Event Details

Online Registration Closing Registration by 9th april (First 40 Participants only!)
Preliminary Round: Offine Gamified Assessment on April 11th (12:30pm - 1:30pm)
Prelims Venue: Department of Banking Technology
Final Round Duration: 40 minutes (10 minutes per participant) on stage
Final Round Venue: JN Auditorium

Final Round Details

Image Generation Task

The top 4 participants will compete on stage. Each participant will be given a reference image and will have 10 minutes to generate a similar image using Bing Image Creator. The evaluation will focus on three key aspects: accuracy (how closely the generated image matches the reference) and speed (how quickly the task is completed)

Evaluation Criteria

  • Accuracy: The degree to which the generated image matches the reference image.
  • Speed: The time taken to complete the task, while maintaining high quality.

Digital Banking: Banking For Everyone - A Mobile Photography Contest

About the Event

Digital banking is revolutionizing financial accessibility, bringing cashless transactions to local vendors, small businesses, and even elderly citizens. This event invites participants to capture powerful moments showcasing how digital banking is transforming lives - whether it's a street vendor using QR codes for payments or senior citizens adapting to mobile banking.

This mobile photography contest aims to visually depict the real-world impact of fintech in bridging financial gaps and reaching underserved communities.
Participants: individuals

Event Guidelines

  • Theme: Digital Banking: Banking For Everyone
  • Participants must submit a mobile photograph that captures the essence of digital banking's outreach - such as local vendors using QR codes, elderly individuals engaging with online transactions, or any scenario highlighting digital financial inclusion.

How to Participate:

    Step 1: Submission
  • Upload your photograph via the Google Form before the deadline: April 10th 2025.
  • Ensure the image adheres to the event's theme. Only mobile photography is allowed (no DSLR or edited images).
  • Add a brief story about the photo.
    • Step 2: Selection Process
  • The best two photographs will be selected based on relevance to the theme, creativity, and impact.
    • Step 3: Winner Announcement
  • Selected winners will be informed a day before the event.
  • On event day, the winners will be announced on stage and awarded cash prizes.
